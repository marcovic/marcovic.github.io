    "The best end to end tool for managing a project-based agency"
    Overall: As a marketing agency, our main issue was the inability of most project management solutions to integrate with our invoicing and financial data, requiring us quite a bit of work to understand what projects and which areas were driving most of the profit for the company. With wethod, we found a fully integrated solution which includes a basic CRM (good enough for our needs), a full-fledged project and task management tool, and provides financial data both aggregated (company P&L) and split at the individual project level. Wethod also has a handy "desk" area where alerts are displayed, letting the project manager know if there is something that needs their attention.
    Pros: There is pretty much no other tool on the market that allows you to track every aspect of a project-based company, from the first economic offer to the final invoice, including time tracking, task management and company financials. We tried Monday, Asana, ClickUp and a bunch of others before landing on Wethod, which has been with us for 3+ years now.
    Cons: While I love the software, the UI could be a bit better, and there is a bit of a learning curve to it.
    Reasons for Choosing WETHOD: It's possibly the only tool designed for a project-based company, which doesn't require cross-referencing different softwares to get a good grip on overall company performance.
