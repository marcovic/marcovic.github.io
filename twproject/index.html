"Work Accomplishment Facilitated; An Actual "Operating System""
Overall: Our small group has used and has advocated for TWProject for several years. TWProject is a fully coordinated work operating system. TWProject elegantly and simultaneously accommodates any/all of project, process, scrum, kanban, and issue/response-based interaction among user/work participants in actual work-construct time. The work operating system manages each/all individual and combined resource capacities and time constraints NOT as a planning, or as a disconnected model, but as actual logged-on system resources. Additionally TWProject provides literally dozens of tracking, communicative and reporting functions that provide extreme operational value to our teams who depend on this fine system. And the TWProject customer support tools/staff have been great. Of all the tools I personally use/recommend, often my highest recommendations go to TWProject. It's a great system.
Pros: - Enables the management of work in every way imaginable. - Ease of simple use, but capable of very complicated work coordination. - Logical reporting (limited to the most 'operative/meaningful work info) - Easy to adapt/extend - Inexpensive TCO - Customer support and documentation
Cons: - Non-standard underlying technology - (never a real issue because integration capabilities built into the system are pretty well-thought out/capable)

Pros: Il prodotto è completo, copre molte funzionalità, ritengo utile e fatta molto bene la gestione dei costi e quindi del controllo del budget di un progetto e relativa marginalità.
